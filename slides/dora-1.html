Industry Standard

DORA Metrics.

DORA — backed by Google — is the industry standard for measuring software delivery performance. Over 10 years of research across 36,000+ teams proves that speed and stability are not tradeoffs. The best teams excel at both.

Throughput

Change Lead Time

Time from code commit to running in production. Shorter = faster delivery.

Deployment Frequency

How often you ship to production. Higher = more responsive to users.

Stability

Change Fail Rate

Percentage of deployments that cause a failure. Lower = higher quality releases.

Failed Deployment Recovery Time

How long it takes to restore service after an incident. Faster = more resilient.

Elite teams deploy on demand, with lead times under one hour and change fail rates below 5%.
